ELEC3848 IDP - Web based Car Controller v0.3

About This App

This web app controls your Arduino car over Web Serial and shows the latest A0 reading in real time.

Bluetooth reminder: Pair your Bluetooth module in system settings first (Windows/macOS/Linux). After pairing, open Serial Port and you should see the paired device in the port list with your modified module name or as SPP Dev.
